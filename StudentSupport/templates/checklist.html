Essential Next Steps

Secure your spot by completing the required deposit payment. This is mandatory to confirm your admission.

Instructions:

  • Review payment instructions on the NEU Registration & Payment Details page.
  • Complete your payment by the deadline: September 10, 2025.
  • Save your receipt for your records.

Find and secure your dorm or housing option. Ensure your accommodation is confirmed before arrival.

Instructions:

  • Explore available housing options on the NEU Accommodation page.
  • Complete registration forms and optionally use the Roommate Finder to match with a roommate.
  • Submit forms to the housing office by September 15, 2025.

Attend the orientation sessions to familiarize yourself with campus and university life.

Instructions:

  • Check orientation schedules on the NEU Orientation Program page.
  • Participate in all required sessions to understand university resources and campus navigation.

Important Dates:

  • September 21: Orientation week begins
  • September 25: Orientation sessions end

Activate your NEU student email account to receive official communications.

Instructions:

  • Follow the step-by-step guide on the NEU Email Setup page.
  • Ensure your account is active and you can access your inbox.

Obtain your student ID card, essential for accessing campus facilities.

Instructions:

  • Visit the NEU Student Affairs Office.
  • Bring a valid photo ID and your student registration details.
  • Complete required forms and submit to the office staff.

Engage with the university community by joining clubs and accessing student services.

Instructions:

  • Explore available clubs on the NEU Student Clubs page.
  • Attend the club fair to meet members and learn about opportunities.
