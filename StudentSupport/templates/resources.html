Resources at Near East University

Here you’ll find the essential services, facilities, and contacts to help you settle in and thrive during your time at NEU.

Academic Support

Students can access their courses, grades, and schedules through the Student Information System (SIS). Academic advising is available in every faculty to help plan your degree path. NEU also offers the EINDROID mobile app for managing academic information on-the-go.

The Grand Library at NEU is one of the largest in the Eastern Mediterranean, open 24/7. It holds over 2.25 million printed books, 60,000+ e-journals, and thousands of digital resources accessible worldwide. The library also provides quiet study halls, group workspaces, amphitheaters for academic events, and rare collections. Students can also use the Student Information System to access course materials, grades, and schedules. Academic advising services are available in every faculty to help plan your degree path.

Health & Wellness

NEU provides a fully equipped hospital on campus, providing general health, emergency services, and specialized care to students. A student health center offers check-ups, vaccinations, and medical guidance. Mental health and counseling services are available for personal, emotional, and academic support. Fitness and wellness are encouraged through the Olympic swimming pool, gyms, sports arenas, and walking paths on campus.

Campus Life

Campus life at NEU is vibrant and diverse. Students can choose from over 150 clubs and organizations covering culture, arts, sports, volunteering, and entrepreneurship. Learn more about clubs here. The campus includes cafeterias, restaurants, banks, supermarkets, post office, hairdressers, bookstores, and guesthouses for visitors. NEU also organizes regular events, including cultural festivals, concerts, conferences, and student fairs, ensuring that there is always something to take part in outside the classroom.

Technology & Learning Tools

Students have access to computer labs, high-speed campus Wi-Fi, and IT support desks to assist with technical issues. Online resources like the NEU Learning Management System allow students to download lecture notes, watch recorded sessions, and submit assignments digitally. The library’s digital portal allows remote access to academic journals, e-books, and research databases. Free software licenses are also provided for popular academic tools such as Microsoft Office.

Financial & Scholarships

NEU provides a wide range of scholarships, including 50% tuition waivers for all international students, as well as academic merit scholarships and sports scholarships. Payment plans are available to spread tuition across installments. Students may also apply for part-time on-campus jobs through the student affairs office. Guidance on financial aid, budgeting, and sponsorship programs is available through the Student Affairs Office. Learn more here. Financial aid services are available for guidance on tuition payments, budgeting, and sponsorship programs.

Emergency & Safety

NEU maintains a 24/7 campus security team to ensure the safety of all students. Emergency services are directly connected with the NEU Hospital, which is located on campus. Students should save these contacts for urgent needs:

  • Campus Security (Emergency): +90 (392) 223 64 64
  • NEU Hospital: +90 (392) 680 20 00
  • General University Info: info@neu.edu.tr

For emergencies outside the university, Northern Cyprus has nationwide numbers:

  • General Emergency: 112
  • Police (Lefkoşa, Girne, Famagusta): 155
  • Fire Brigade: 199
  • Ambulance: 112
  • Forest Fire Hotline: 177

Fire stations and first-aid units are also located within the campus. In addition, the university regularly conducts safety drills and awareness workshops.
